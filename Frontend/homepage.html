Welcome to BirbyDB

Explore and contribute to the fascinating world of bird observation.

How to use our database

1. Create an account

2. Search our current database for any bird of your liking to view it's name, last seen location and last seen time.

3. Create your own observations to be added into our Californian database.

4. Go to your profile to make your own lists of birds (ex:] Wishlist of birds you would like to see or yourself!)

5. Connect and explore with our community of bird watchers and enjoyers, and fly on!
