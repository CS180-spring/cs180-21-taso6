Welcome to BirbyDB

Explore and contribute to the fascinating world of bird observation.

What Is It?

BirbyDB is an online database platform designated to sharing and discovering bird observations from around the state of California.

Who Is It For?

This website is for bird enthusiasts and nature observers of all levels of interest, providing a space for learning, sharing, and exploring the world of birds.

Why Did We Make It?

We wanted to create a user-friendly and inclusive space for bird enthusiasts to foster a sense of community and collective knowledge building.

How to use our database

1. Create an account

2. Search our current database for any bird of your liking to view its name, last seen location, and last seen time.

3. Create your own observations to be added into our Californian database.

4. Go to your profile to make your own lists of birds (e.g., Wishlist of birds you would like to see for yourself!)

5. Connect and explore with our community of bird watchers and enjoyers, and fly on!
